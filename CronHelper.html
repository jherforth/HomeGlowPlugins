Minutes and hours are ignored - chores only trigger once daily!

Advanced Cron Patterns:
  • * = Every (e.g., every day, every month)
  • */7 = Every 7 units (e.g., every 7 days)
  • 2/7 = Offset step (2nd day, then every 7 days)
  • 1-5 = Range (e.g., Monday through Friday)
  • 2#4 = Nth weekday (2nd Thursday of month)
